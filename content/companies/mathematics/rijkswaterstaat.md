Rijkswaterstaat is the executive agency of the Ministry of Infrastructure and Water Management and works every day towards a safe, livable, and accessible Netherlands. We manage and develop national highways, waterways, bridges, tunnels, and flood defenses.

Within Rijkswaterstaat, thousands of professionals collaborate on major societal challenges such as mobility, water safety, and climate adaptation. Technology, data, and innovation are playing an increasingly important role in this work.

During this one-on-one meetings, we will share more about the work of Rijkswaterstaat and the opportunities available for students and recent graduates.
