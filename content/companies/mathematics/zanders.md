Zanders is a global financial consulting firm, deploying best in class minds, models and technology. We help our clients see unseen risks and capture hidden opportunities. Since our founding in 1994, we 
have been dedicated to providing our clients with innovative and practical solutions to their financial challenges. Over the three decades, Zanders has ensured that our clients are the victors rather than 
the victims of change, translating preparedness into the ability to propel their organizations, economies and the world forward. We believe that by combining our deep expertise with a personalized 
approach, we can help our clients achieve their goals and optimize their financial performance.
 
The financial sector is facing significant challenges due to low interest rates, the rise of fintech, and the growing complexity of regulatory requirements. New technologies are transforming the market, 
and profitability remains under pressure, creating an environment of heightened uncertainty.
 
Our Risk Advisory team combines deep expertise in financial regulations and advanced modelling with a strategic approach to risk management. We empower financial institutions to design and implement 
resilient risk frameworks that ensure regulatory compliance while preparing for future challenges and meeting stakeholder expectations.
 
From forecasting accuracy to model risk management and stress testing, our consultants utilize their expertise in using AI and machine learning to analyze large volumes of data more rapidly and with 
significantly less need for human intervention.
